OPENING EVENTS

World of Coffee Beans: Tasting Event

Travel the globe through your taste buds! Explore the unique flavor profiles of coffee beans from different regions, such as Ethiopian Yirgacheffe, Colombian Supremo, and Indonesian Sumatra. Learn how terroir and processing methods influence the taste.

Duration: 1.5 hours

From Farm to Cup: Coffee Bean Origins Talk

A fascinating journey into the life of a coffee bean. This event features a presentation by a coffee expert or farmer, highlighting how beans are grown, harvested, processed, and exported. Learn about fair trade practices and sustainability efforts in the coffee industry.

Duration: 1 hour

OPENING WORKSHOPS

Home Brewing Masterclass

Learn how to brew café-quality coffee in the comfort of your home. This class covers the basics of different brewing methods, including pour-over, French press, AeroPress, and cold brew. Attendees will get hands-on experience using professional-grade equipment and take home a guide with brewing ratios, tips, and tricks.

Duration: 2 hours

  • Guided brewing demonstrations by expert baristas
  • Sample tastings of each brewing method
  • Take-home brewing guide
  • Exclusive discounts on coffee beans and brewing tools
